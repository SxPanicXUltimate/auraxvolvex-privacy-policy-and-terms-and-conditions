AURAXVOLVEX respects your privacy. Here is how we handle your data.

We do not collect personal data like name, email, or phone numbers.

  • Each device is assigned an 'Anonymous Identity'.
  • Your ID is automatically deleted from our server after 30 days of inactivity.

We only collect technical data (e.g., crash reports) to improve app performance.
